Principal Message

Our college is committed to academic excellence, skill development, and shaping future-ready, responsible citizens.

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to Government Polytechnic Rajgarh, Mirzapur — a temple of technical learning. Our aim is to provide students with quality education, practical knowledge, and strong moral values that shape them into responsible professionals. With a team of dedicated faculty members, modern infrastructure, and a supportive learning environment, we strive to nurture innovation, discipline, and leadership among students. We believe that technical education should not only create skilled engineers but also inspire individuals to contribute meaningfully to society. Let us work together to build a brighter future, driven by knowledge, passion, and purpose. Wishing all our students great success in life.

Warm regards,
Er. Basant Lal
Principal
Government Polytechnic Rajgarh, Mirzapur
